What is Presto Hacks?

Presto Hacks is a 3 day virtual event (specifically, a hackathon) where teams of 1-4 participants are given 40 hours to code a web or mobile application under the theme of music technology. After the 40 hour period, the projects will be judged based on a set criteria, and the group with the best project in each category will receive a prize.

When/Where:

The event will be held over Discord and Zoom on December 10-12th, 2021. More details concerning schedule, operations, and safety will be posted closer to the event on the website. With any questions about these details, contact info@prestohacks.tech.

Eligibility

Anyone aged 13-22!

Our Mission:

Presto Hacks has 3 primary goals:

  1. To discover new ways people can engage with and experience music through programming and technology.
  2. To improve access to music education, especially in underserved communities.
  3. To connect the worlds of music and technology.
